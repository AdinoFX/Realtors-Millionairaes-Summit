About the Realtors Millionaire Summit (RMS)

The Realtors Millionaire Summit (RMS) is an initiative by SAKWES, the realtors arm of the Esso Group, aimed at empowering real estate professionals with insights, strategies, and a millionaire mindset to achieve extraordinary success.

Designed to kickstart the year with a vision of excellence, RMS provides participants with access to top industry experts, training, and motivation. This summit not only equips attendees with effective sales techniques but also serves as a powerful networking platform.
